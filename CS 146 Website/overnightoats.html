Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Rolled Oats (Cups) 0.5
Milk (dairy or plant-based; cups) 0.5
Salt (teaspoons) 0.0625

Instructions:

  1. Combine rolled oats and milk (dairy or plant-based) in a jar.
  2. Add sweetener (like honey or maple syrup), salt, and your favorite toppings (fruits, nuts, seeds).
  3. Stir well, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  4. In the morning, give it a good mix, and your creamy overnight oats are ready to enjoy!
